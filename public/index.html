About the company

Built in Kismayo. Trusted across Jubaland.

● Family business since 2015

Prime General Supplies & Services Company is a leading general trading company based in the Federal Republic of Somalia. Since our founding as a family business in 2015, we have established and maintained strong working relationships with clients and suppliers — not only to provide responsive, reliable products and services, but to constantly exceed expectations.

From our headquarters in Kismayo, we supply humanitarian and institutional partners with goods, construction works, and logistics services throughout Jubaland, Bay Region, Banadir, and beyond — backed by a track record of signed, delivered, and accepted contracts.

Our Mission

To attract and retain customers by providing the best in construction, supply, and transportation solutions — fostering a disciplined culture of safety, service, and trust.

Our Vision

To be recognised as the leading company in construction, general logistics, and supplies in Somalia.

What we do

Four capabilities, one dependable contractor.

From office supplies to shallow wells, we cover the full scope humanitarian and institutional projects need on the ground in Somalia.

01

General Trading & Supply

Office furniture, NFI & hygiene kits, equipment, and institutional goods — sourced, delivered, and documented to donor standards.

02

Construction & Works

Classrooms, latrines, shelters, reservoirs, and institutional buildings — built to BoQ and design specification, on schedule.

03

WASH & Water Infrastructure

Shallow wells, boreholes, solar-powered water pumps, and sanitation facilities for schools, IDP sites, and communities.

04

Logistics & Transportation

Vehicle rental, food and cargo transportation, and project M&E support across Jubaland and neighbouring regions.
